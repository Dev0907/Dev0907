### 📄 [Comprehensive Analysis of Bilingual Text Extraction: OCR Techniques for Gujarati and English](https://link.springer.com/chapter/10.1007/978-981-96-4139-0_26)

**Dev Parikh**, Nikita Bhatt, Dhaval Bhoi

> 📘 *Springer LNNS Vol. 1323 · pp. 307–316 · ICTCS 2024*
> 🗓️ First Online: **14 August 2025** · 👁️ **125 Accesses**

In-depth analysis of OCR techniques for extracting and distinguishing Gujarati and English text from bilingual documents. Proposes advanced preprocessing, text segmentation, and language identification. Addresses Gujarati script's complex structure, diacritical marks, and conjunct letters — achieving **90.75% (English)** and **91.16% (Gujarati)** extraction accuracy.

### 📄 [Natural Language Processing in Email Spam Filtering: A Comparative Study](https://link.springer.com/chapter/10.1007/978-981-96-4142-0_19)

Jay Parmar, **Dev Parikh**, Amit Thakkar, Dhaval Bhoi

> 📘 *Springer LNNS Vol. 1324 · pp. 225–235 · ICTCS 2024*
> 🗓️ First Online: **01 October 2025** · 👁️ **101 Accesses**

Comparative study applying NLP techniques to email spam classification. Benchmarks FastText against Bag-of-Words + Random Forest using tokenization, stopword removal, and stemming for preprocessing. Demonstrates significant improvement in spam filter resilience and scalability for real-world email security.
